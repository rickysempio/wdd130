Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to share my information about my personal portfolio to show my knowledge, skills and my experiences to convince my potential employer to hire me.

Audience

The site’s intended audience are Business Owners and visitors who are interested and looking with similar knowledge, skills and experiences that I have. Their ages would be between 18-60 years old and they can access my site on their phones, tablets and desktops.

Content

Home page

The homepage would be my introduction to my web page, where I would inform my visitor the purpose of my website to share my personal portfolio, to show my skills, knowledge and experienced. I would be telling them the benefits of coming to my website, that they can learned new skills and knowledge that can help them to be productive as a person or as a decision maker. I would be sharing also a little background about myself as a person and as professional, for them to have idea about myself. I would be displaying an image that describes about my portfolio and another image that describe about myself as a person, so they can have an overview of what is my website all about and to let them see whether their expectations are being meet as they are coming to my website. And eventually make them realized that they were right coming to my website and satisfy their purposes.

Images for the Home page

Hire Me

On this page I would like to highlight why they should be hiring me, I would show my qualifications, that I have the knowledge, and the skills they are looking for, I would show some achievements to support my qualifications, and share a motivating principles that actually motivated and helped me to overcome some obstacles to show to my future employee that its not only the knowledge and skills that you know that would make you to succeed in your endeavour, but also those life lessons that would cheer you up dealing with some obstacles along the way as you do your endeavours in work or in life. On this page I would display an image showing my skills and projects that I have done, to let my visitor and my potential employer have an idea and see, what I can do by looking at those images, and to finally convince them that I am also one of those pieces that they need that it takes them to be successful too in their endeavours or goals in their respective organizations.

Images for the Page 2

My Contact

On this part of the page, it will contain all my contacts for me to be reached by my website visitors and my potential employer, they can reach me via my email, my social media accounts or even my handphone. I will also create section for interaction between me and my visitors like comments section. On this page I would be displaying an image that supports my contacts and another showing interaction with my visitors.
